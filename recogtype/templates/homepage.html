Some common Q&A before you register:

What is RecogType?

The short answer is that RecogType is a prototype for a data collection and recognition system created to support my MSc thesis at the University of Kent. The title of my thesis is "An Online Evaluation Platform for Keystroke Dynamics Backed Recognition" which basically means my goal was to create an online system that can be reused no matter how recognition is done on the back-end but data will always be collected the same on the front.

Who can use RecogType?

As long as you are 18 or older you can use RecogType! No matter your typing style or skill you are welcome to join in the study.

What are my responsibilities as a user?

As a user, you will be challenged to type on your keyboard as you normally would but in different scenarios. For example, one challenge may ask you to simply type an excerpt of a book that has been provided to you. Another challenge may be to type up a mock email to a friend. The goal is to get you typing naturally and extract your data.

What do you do with my data?

Whenever you supply us with your keystroke data it is immediately and securely saved in our project database. Your keystroke information is then studied to find out how accurately we can model your typing style and how unique your typing style is to you.

How long will you keep my data for?

Your account information will be kept until the end of this project, which concludes in mid-September, no matter what. Your keystroke information will be kept until the end of the research project as well. There may be the possibility that future research is conducted on the data provided, in this case you will be given the option whether or not to allow us to retain your data. If you allow us to retain your data it will be stripped of your email, password and first name making it 100% anonymous.

How can I withdraw from the study?

If you've created an account and decided you would like to opt out that is not a problem. Simply email me at and it will be taken care of immediately. Whether you've completed 20 challenges or just recently created an account your information will be wiped clean and your account removed from our system.

Where Can I Learn More?

Within the navigation bar at the top you can find a link to the FAQs page which will delve deeper into the project and give you, the user, the ability to email me with other questions you may have. This project also aims to enlighten the user community on the technologies it is built-on so the more questions the better!
